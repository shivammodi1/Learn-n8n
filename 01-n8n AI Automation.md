# What is AI?
- AI means smart sofware that can think and learn from data.
- It can understand things like text, images, and sounds.
- Examples:
    - Youtube recommending videos.
    - Chatbots answering questions.
    - Phone unlock with face recognition.

# What is Automation?
- Aumation means machinees do repeated work for us.
- It saves time and reduces mistakes.
- Examples:
    - Email filters sorting messages.
    - Automatic bill payments.
    - Factory robots assembling products.

# What is AI-Automation?
- It is combining AI and Automation.
- Where AI makes smart decisions and Automation decides what tasks to do.
- Examples:
    - AI chatbots that answer customer questions automatically.
    - Smart email systems that sort and reply to messages.
    - AI tools that analyze data and create reports without human help.

# What AI Agents?
- Ai agents is a digital worker that can do tasks for you using AI.
- They can understand instructions, make decisions, and learn from experience.
- Examples:
    - Virtual assistants like Siri or Alexa.
    - Customer support bots that help with questions.
    - AI tools that manage schedules and reminders.
