# Gmail AI Auto Reply Workflow

## Step 1: Gmail Trigger
- Jab bhi Gmail me new email aata hai, workflow automatically start ho jata hai.

## Step 2: HTML Extract
- Email ka HTML content extract karke readable text me convert karta hai.

## Step 3: AI Agent
- AI Agent email content ko analyze karta hai aur reply generate karta hai.

## Step 4: Ollama AI Model
- Yeh AI model hai jo email ko samajh ke smart response banata hai.

## Step 5: Send Message (Gmail)
- AI Sender ka Summary Gmail and uska draft owner ko dono sath me send karta hai.

## Summary
1. New email aata hai
2. Content extract hota hai
3. AI usko analyze karta hai
4. Response generate hota hai
5. Reply draft owner ko send hota hai
